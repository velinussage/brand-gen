# Content Card Platform Specs

## Dimensions by platform

| Platform | Best ratio | Resolution | Max slides | Safe zone |
|----------|-----------|------------|------------|-----------|
| Instagram carousel | 4:5 | 1080×1350 | 20 | 250px from top/bottom |
| LinkedIn carousel | 4:5 or 1:1 | 1080×1350 or 1080×1080 | 10-12 | 200px from top/bottom |
| X/Twitter feed | 16:9 or 1:1 | 1600×900 or 1080×1080 | 4 | 100px margins |
| Facebook carousel | 1:1 | 1080×1080 | 10 | 200px margins |
| Blog header | 16:9 or 4:5 | 1200×630 or 1080×1350 | 1 | full bleed ok |

## Typography rules for content cards

### Minimum sizes (for mobile readability)
- **Headline**: 36pt+ display font, can be 48-72pt for hook slides
- **Subhead**: 24-28pt, accent color or bold weight
- **Body text**: 22-24pt minimum, regular weight
- **Bullet items**: 22pt, with colored bullet markers (brand accent)
- **CTA text**: 20-24pt, with "→" arrow

### Hierarchy patterns
1. **Typography-only card**: Headline dominates, body text below, brand mark anchored
2. **Text + photo inset**: Text fills 70% of card, rounded photo circle in corner
3. **Text + illustration**: Branded illustration at top, text block below
4. **List card**: Subhead + bullet list, clean whitespace
5. **Stat highlight**: One big number + context sentence

### Font role mapping
- **Headline**: Use display/heading font (e.g., Fraunces for Sage)
- **Subhead**: Use body font in bold + brand accent color
- **Body text**: Use body font (e.g., Inter) at regular weight
- **Step numbers**: Display font, large size, italic if brand supports it
- **CTA**: Body font, regular or medium weight

## Carousel narrative arc

| Slide | Role | Design treatment |
|-------|------|-----------------|
| 1 | Hook | One oversized headline, minimal text, high contrast |
| 2 | Empathy | Validate the pain point, 2-3 sentences |
| 3-6 | Value | Deliver key info, one point per slide |
| 7 | Proof | Stats, testimonials, or case study |
| 8 | CTA | Clear call-to-action + brand mark |

## Color treatment

- **Solid field cards**: Single brand color background, white or cream text
- **Light field cards**: Cream/white background, brand accent for headlines
- **Split cards**: Color field top half, photo bottom half
- **Gradient cards**: Subtle brand gradient (avoid harsh transitions)

## Photo inset patterns

- **Circle inset**: Rounded circle (200-300px), positioned bottom-right or bottom-left
- **Half-bleed**: Photo takes bottom 30-40% of card, text above
- **Background**: Full-bleed photo with text overlay on semi-transparent brand color
- **None**: Pure typography card (strongest for hook/editorial slides)
