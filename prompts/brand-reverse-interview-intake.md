# Brand Reverse Interview Intake

Use this before the first paid generation batch.

## Core questions
1. What brand material are we working on first?
2. What must stay recognizable?
3. What feels wrong or weak today?
4. How far can we move: polish, evolve, or redesign?
5. Where will this appear first?
6. What 3-5 adjectives should the new direction feel like?
7. What should it definitely not feel like?
8. What would count as a clear win in 7 days?
